Our Prices

  • "Greenfield" Development: $105 / hour, billed twice per month
  • Support & Maintenance: $205 / hour, billed twice per month

Mershon Enterprises does business the Bakersfield way. While we're happy to offer maintenance and support services for software written by us or your previous developer, we don't believe in indefinitely entangling our business with yours.

For any development project, you own a full, unabridged copy of all source code, object code, technical documentation, user documentation, and supporting code developed for your project which cannot be revoked even if you decide to quit using our services mid-project and work with another agency. Unlike many developers, freelance and agency alike, we do not use a "Software as a Service" pricing model for the products we build, which would require you to pay a monthly fee indefinitely.

Instead, we offer a straightforward pricing model: all proposals and invoices are based on hours worked, and we limit your risk by capping each phase of a project as Time & Materials, Not to Exceed. This means the price on the paper is the biggest number you will ever see, for the full scope and duration of the project, and if we take less time to get your project finished, you reap the savings.
