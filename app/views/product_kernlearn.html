Kern Learn

White-label solution for free and paid educational courses.

Kern Learn is a Clojure and ClojureScript web application designed as a consumer-facing platform for hosting free and paid courses for secondary education. It is unique in that it allows for convenient posting of multi-session, multi-venue courses with varying times and durations for a single price per unit. Payment processing is facilitated through Stripe.

Kern Learn is proprietary to Mershon Enterprises and available as a paid white-label solution for businesses offering educational opportunities.

You can see a live, in-production version of KernLearn used to offer courses for Mesh Cowork at KernLearn.io.
