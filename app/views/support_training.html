Our Project Methodology

Mershon Enterprises employs a number of Project Controls methodologies to ensure that each project is delivered on time, on budget, and within scope. These methods include Agile (SCRUM) and the more conventional critical path method.

Our project schedules are all loaded with cost and labor hours for the purposes of earned value reporting and analysis. With this data, we report on budget and schedule issues as they arise, and find strategic solutions before projects are at risk. We also offer project administration services a la carte, including conversion between formats.
