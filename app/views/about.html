Project Administrative Controls

Mershon Enterprises employs a number of Project Controls methodologies to ensure that each project is delivered on time, on budget, and within scope. These methods include Agile, Scrum, and the more conventional critical path method. Our project schedules are all loaded with cost and labor hours for the purposes of earned value reporting and analysis. With this data, we report on budget and schedule issues as they arise, and find strategic solutions before projects are at risk. We also offer project administration services a la carte, including conversion between formats.

Benefits of a Virtual Company

Your business needs aren't constrained to your desk or your office, and your software solutions shouldn't be either. Being a virtual company means our developers build lightweight, efficient software out of necessity. Portable software is better for your business because it allows you to ask questions and get answers on-the-go, even with intermittent connectivity.

Coworking and Mesh Bakersfield

Being a virtual company doesn't mean our programmers work in isolation. Mershon Enterprises was instrumental in helping to found Mesh Cowork, Bakersfield's first coworking facility. Together with other entrepreneurs, freelance software developers, and inspired thinkers, we are building a better Bakersfield. If you're local, please join us by visiting the space, and supporting your local developer community.
